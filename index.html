Safety

Plan ahead ‚Äî you're on the ocean

Paddling on Lambert Channel is all about wind and tides. Here's what every paddler should know before launching.

üå¨Ô∏è

Check Wind Conditions

Get predictions via Marine Weather ‚Äî North of Nanaimo. Wind can be over- or under-forecast for Denman specifically.

üìä

Check Tide Heights

Use Fisheries Canada Tides & Water Levels ‚Äî search for Hornby Island Tide Heights for our location.

‚ö†Ô∏è

Don't Launch at Very Low Tide

Our rental boats are heavy. Launching below a 12-foot tide can be slippery with dangerous exposed barnacles. Plan your timing carefully.

üïê

Island Time is Real

The ferry often runs off schedule with line-ups in spring and summer. Build in buffer time, especially if you're coming from off-island.
